What are airdrops and why are they needed?

Airdrops are free distributions this text for test

Airdrops are free distributions of cryptocurrency to community members. The main purposes of airdrops are:

  • Project promotion: Attracting new users.
  • Community support: Rewarding loyalty.
  • Providing liquidity: Creating demand for a new cryptocurrency.

By participating in airdrops, you can receive tokens for free. Usually, participation requires completing simple tasks, such as following the project's social media or registering on the platform.
